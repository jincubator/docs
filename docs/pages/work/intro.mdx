# Work

Selected Work explains what was built, who owns the implementation, what the evidence establishes, and where the current boundary stops.

## Salus

[Salus](/work/salus) is active trading-system research and a reference implementation. The case study separates market state, route catalogs, evaluation, selection, execution, and realized outcomes while keeping dated measurements beside their limitations.

## Digital Banking

[Digital Banking](/work/digital-banking) is a deliberately non-production reference implementation for durable settlement control planes. It demonstrates exact-value workflows, local Ethereum and Solana effects, accounting evidence, restart recovery, and reconciliation without claiming a bank deployment or production certification.

## Intent Systems Prototypes

[Intent Systems Prototypes](/work/prototypes) consolidates the UHI5 hook and 2025 Unite DeFi solver experiments. It records the bounded implementation lessons while distinguishing completed prototype behavior from roadmaps, partner ideas, and production claims.

Historical initiatives whose contribution, disclosure, rights, or evidence boundaries remain unresolved are available through the secondary [Archive](/archive/intro), not presented as current selected Work.
