# Partnerships

## How to Engage with Jincubator

Jincubator is a research and innovation studio exploring technical systems through research, architecture, prototypes, products, and venture formation.

Partnerships help transform research into real-world outcomes through collaboration, product development, funding, and ecosystem participation.

---

## Research Partnerships

Research partnerships focus on exploring emerging technologies, validating ideas, and developing reference architectures.

Areas of interest include:

- Trading Systems
- Financial Infrastructure
- DeFi Protocol Engineering
- Distributed Systems
- AI Engineering

Examples include protocol research, architecture design, technical investigations, and proof-of-concept development.

---

## Architecture Initiatives

Jincubator develops architecture designs that help organizations understand and evaluate complex technical systems.

Potential collaboration areas include:

- Trading Infrastructure
- Financial Infrastructure
- Payments and Settlement
- Stablecoin Platforms
- Protocol Architecture
- Distributed Systems
- AI Systems

Architecture initiatives may result in reference architectures, design reviews, prototypes, or implementation roadmaps.

## Funding & Venture Formation

Jincubator is interested in opportunities that help transform validated research into sustainable products and ventures.
