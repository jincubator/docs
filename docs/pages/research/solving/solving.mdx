# High-Performance Route Evaluation

John's active research uses retained replay measurements from July 9, 2026 to separate graph scale and route-evaluation throughput from opportunity quality, execution, and realized outcomes.

## Definitions

- A **generated route** is a candidate graph path.
- An **evaluated route** is one assessment under a particular state and model.
- A **filtered opportunity** is a candidate that passes named research gates.
- **Simulated profit** is a model result, not settled value.
- A **submitted or included execution** is a transaction lifecycle event.
- **Realized after-gas profit** is settled economic value after costs and failures.
