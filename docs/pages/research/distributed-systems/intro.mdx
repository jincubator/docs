# Distributed Systems

Distributed Systems collects research on chains, bridges, zero knowledge systems, cryptographic primitives, data availability, and cross-chain infrastructure.

Much of this material is historical research from earlier Jincubator work, but it remains useful context for architecture, protocol engineering, settlement, and product design.

## Current Focus

- Chain architecture and consensus systems
- Bridges, cross-chain messaging, and light clients
- Zero knowledge systems and proving infrastructure
- Cryptographic primitives
- Data availability
