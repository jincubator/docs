# DeFi Protocol Engineering

DeFi Protocol Engineering covers protocol design, intents, hooks, liquidity infrastructure, solver participation, and integrations with ecosystems such as Uniswap, 1inch, and Tycho.

This is one of the strongest current research areas on the site. The existing work spans intent architecture, Uniswap v4 hooks, liquidity and resource management, solver execution, Tycho-backed route evaluation, and 1inch contract development.

## Current Focus

- Intents and resource management
- Uniswap v4 hooks and execution patterns
- 1inch and Fusion contract research
- Tycho integration and market-data-backed routing
- Liquidity infrastructure and solver participation
