# Settlement

Settlement research focuses on how obligations become final across payment systems, stablecoin platforms, intent systems, and cross-chain infrastructure.

This area connects Circle CCTP, Eco-style fulfillment and proving flows, resource locking, stablecoin movement, cross-chain verification, and the operational guarantees required by financial applications.

## Current Focus

- Cross-chain settlement and message verification
- Stablecoin transfer, mint, and burn flows
- Intent fulfillment and resource locking
- Finality, replay protection, and reconciliation
- Settlement-aware product architecture
