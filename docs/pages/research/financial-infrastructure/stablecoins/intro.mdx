# Stablecoins

> Historical topic page retained for compatibility. It is not a fresh comparative stablecoin study.

Stablecoin research focuses on token systems used for payments, settlement, liquidity, and financial platform infrastructure.

The retained material is strongest around Circle architecture, stablecoin control surfaces, CCTP, mint and burn flows, proxy upgrades, pausing, blacklisting, permit flows, and the relationship between stablecoins and settlement infrastructure.

## Historical focus

- Stablecoin contract architecture
- Mint, burn, pause, blacklist, and upgrade controls
- CCTP and cross-chain stablecoin movement
- Settlement and reconciliation patterns
- Stablecoin infrastructure for product development
