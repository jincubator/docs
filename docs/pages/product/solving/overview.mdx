# Salus Architecture Overview

Salus is active research and a reference implementation. Its [project overview](/work/salus) explains the retained results and limitations.

The public architecture follows a simple boundary:

1. ingest and normalize market state;
2. maintain graph and candidate-route views;
3. evaluate affected routes and apply named gates;
4. simulate separately from submission or inclusion;
5. record evidence without equating a signal with realized value.

Current provider policy, fees, thresholds, prioritization, route selection, opportunity values, and submission strategy are not public operating instructions.

Continue to the [Salus architecture](/architecture/trading-systems/salus) for the deeper system view.
