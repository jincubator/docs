# Architecture

Architecture is where research becomes system design.

Jincubator uses architecture work to make complex systems easier to evaluate, build, and productize. These pages focus on boundaries, components, data flows, execution paths, operational controls, and implementation tradeoffs across technical domains.

The site now separates three related views:

- **Research**: what has been learned.
- **Architecture**: how systems are designed.
- **Product**: what is being built.

This section is organized around solution areas rather than research topics.

## Architecture Areas

- [Trading Systems](/architecture/trading-systems/intro): trading infrastructure, route evaluation, execution, market data, and solver-to-product architecture.
- [Financial Infrastructure](/architecture/financial-infrastructure/intro): payments, stablecoin systems, settlement, APIs, enterprise integration, SOA platforms, and institutional financial platforms.
- [DeFi Systems](/architecture/defi-systems/intro): intents, hooks, liquidity infrastructure, protocol execution, and solver participation.
- [Distributed Systems](/architecture/distributed-systems/intro): chains, bridges, proving systems, consensus boundaries, and cross-chain infrastructure.
- [Enterprise Architecture](/architecture/enterprise-architecture/intro): platform design, integration systems, product architecture, and production-readiness review.
- [AI Systems](/architecture/ai-systems/intro): AI-assisted engineering, research tooling, agent systems, and automation patterns.
