# Reference Architecture: FalconX

> Historical independent public-source study. It does not imply endorsement, commission, internal access, or implementation ownership, and it is not current canonical Architecture.

This wrapper keeps FalconX architecture in the Architecture section while preserving the canonical research dossier.

FalconX is a reference architecture, not a Jincubator-built system. It is useful as an institutional trading-platform architecture lens: client connectivity, RFQ and RFS flows, prime brokerage surfaces, custody, financing, settlement, reporting, and market-infrastructure boundaries.

## Architecture Lens

- Client connectivity across REST, WebSocket, and FIX.
- RFQ, RFS, reporting, balances, transfers, and account surfaces.
- Custody, financing, DMA, off-exchange settlement, and institutional product boundaries.
- Responsible inference around OMS, EMS, venue connectivity, risk, credit, margin, settlement, market data, and reporting.
