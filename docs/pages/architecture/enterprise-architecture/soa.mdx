# Enterprise SOA

> Historical presentation retained for compatibility. It is not a current ownership or client-delivery claim.

Enterprise SOA covers service-oriented architecture, enterprise integration, partner-system workflows, and long-lived platform boundaries.

This page keeps the enterprise architecture diagrams in a specific Architecture page instead of overloading the Enterprise Architecture intro.

Enterprise SOA is also part of the [Financial Infrastructure Architecture](/architecture/financial-infrastructure/intro) story when service boundaries involve payments, settlement, licensing, e-commerce, logistics, manufacturing, SAP, Salesforce, or other enterprise platform integrations.
